Events in Washington

Seafair

Date: July - August

Location: Seattle, Washington

Description: Seafair is an annual summer festival in Seattle that features a variety of events, including parades, hydro plane races, air shows, and community celebrations. It is one of the largest and most popular festivals in the Pacific Northwest, attracting hundreds of thousands of visitors each year.

Bumbershoot

Date: Labor Day Weekend (September)

Location: Seattle Center, Seattle, Washington

Description: Bumbershoot is an annual music and arts festival held at Seattle Center. It features a diverse lineup of musical performances, comedy shows, visual arts, dance, and more. The festival attracts both local and international artists and is known for its eclectic mix of genres and styles.

Fremont Fair

Date: June

Location: Fremont neighborhood, Seattle, Washington

Description: The Fremont Fair is an annual street fair held in the Fremont neighborhood of Seattle. It features live music, food vendors, arts and crafts booths, and a variety of family-friendly activities. The fair is known for its quirky and eclectic atmosphere, reflecting the unique character of the Fremont community.

Washington State Fair

Date: September

Location: Puyallup, Washington

Description: The Washington State Fair, also known as the Puyallup Fair, is one of the largest fairs in the United States. It features a wide range of attractions, including agricultural exhibits, carnival rides, live entertainment, food vendors, and more. The fair celebrates the agricultural heritage of Washington State and provides fun for the whole family.
