Sports Teams

Seattle Seahawks

Location: Seattle, Washington

Description: The Seattle Seahawks are a professional American football team based in Seattle. They compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member club of the league's National Football Conference (NFC) West division. The team was established in 1976 and has won multiple division titles and one Super Bowl championship.

Seattle Mariners

Location: Seattle, Washington

Description: The Seattle Mariners are a professional baseball team based in Seattle. They compete in Major League Baseball (MLB) as a member club of the American League (AL) West division. The team was established in 1977 and has made several playoff appearances, although they have yet to win a World Series championship.

Seattle Sounders FC

Location: Seattle, Washington

Description: Seattle Sounders FC is a professional soccer team based in Seattle. They compete in Major League Soccer (MLS) as a member club of the Western Conference. The team was established in 2009 and has won multiple U.S. Open Cup titles and one MLS Cup championship.

Seattle Orcas

Location: Seattle, Washington

Description: The Seattle Orcas are a professional cricket team based in Seattle. They compete in Major League Cricket (MLC) as a member club of the league. The team was established in 2023 and aims to promote and develop the sport of cricket in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States.
