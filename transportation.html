Transportation

Link Light Rail

Seattle’s Link Light Rail offers a fast and convenient way to travel between major destinations like the airport, downtown, and the University of Washington.

King County Metro Buses

Metro buses are Seattle’s backbone of public transit, covering hundreds of routes that reach neighborhoods across King County.

Washington State Ferries

Ferries are an iconic part of Seattle’s transportation scene, connecting the city to nearby islands and the Olympic Peninsula.

Bike Share and Cycling

Seattle’s bike lanes and trails make it easy to get around on two wheels.

Seattle Water Taxi

The Seattle Water Taxi connects downtown with West Seattle and Vashon Island.

Seattle Streetcar

The modern Seattle Streetcar connects key urban districts like South Lake Union and Capitol Hill.
